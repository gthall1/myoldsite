Brand Ambassador Prospects

There is no doubt that as Trace enters the camera and drone market that you will immediately draw attention via the impressive hardware, but what will catapult growth and drive revenue is content generation. Strong brand ambassadors can act as a catalyst to quickly put your company and product on the map, especially in the early days following market launch. These are a collection of personal friends, acquaintances, and people I admire who have made a name for themselves in the professional world or on social media. I think it's important to have a wide array of talented people across a variety of professions and sports when building a brand ambassador team. Many people in the industry have focused soley on action sports, but there are many other uses for action cameras in traditional sports, photography, and everyday use cases. I beleive it is crucial to show that anyone can use your product to create content to share with the world because this is what will ultimately drive growth and provide the most value. These are some great candidates in my opinion, and this is where I'd start.
