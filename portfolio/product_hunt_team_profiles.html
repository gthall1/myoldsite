Product Hunt team profiles

Between your piece on giving credit to your team and Hunter Walk's latest blog post it is pretty clear Product Hunt is looking to make an emphasis on highlighting individuals and the work they've done. If you're going to put team members in the spotlight I would argue that it is important to allow them to provide the social media avenues by which they prefer to express themselves. Although Twitter may be the go-to for your team or even the majority it would be nice for team members to be able to choose which of their social media accounts they provide. Because listing just your Twitter handle is nice, but as you have shown your writing isn't half bad so give us a link to your Medium page as well! ;)

Yes yes, I know twitter handle is unique identifier and website is also supplied via oauth. I guess this is more of a suggestion to overall user profiles (and pop-up modals), but that's bothered me a few times now and you asked so I figured I'd slip this in now. With the increased emphasis on the individual I'd love to see the capability of user profiles to be a little more built out. If I'm checking out a team member I'd love to have quick access to more of their work instead of Sherlock'in it up for five minutes to find all their stuff around the web. Anyways, great work though; excited about what you guys are building.
