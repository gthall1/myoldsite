Connect Crimson

This is a company we started which spawned from a website we built which was born from an inherent question that my friends and I had: where are the best drink specials in town? Not only did Connect Crimson provide every drink special at every bar every night, but we also published lunch specials, beer prices, a list of restaurants in town based on the current time, and we even had a ticket exchange for students.

Starting, growing, and ultimately selling this company was a great experience, and though I learned a lot through a successful exit I probably learned much more from the failures. I learned how to (and how not to) hire, fire, manage employees, raise capital, manage a product and the different teams involved in its success, and ultimately work with all the parties involved in an acquisition. That just scratches the surface of the list of things you learn by founding a startup, but that opportunity also taught me a lot about myself and I'm very thankful for the experience.

  • Griff Hall
