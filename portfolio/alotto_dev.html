How it works

the most simple breakdown is:
- user signs up
- user earns credits by playing game
- user can deposit credits into "pot"
- once pot is full, 1 credit/ticket is drawn from the pot and that user is winner of prize

- a "credit" is essentially like a name written on a piece of paper
- more "credits" you earn, the more tickets you have in the pot/opportunities to win the drawing

- your credits belong to you. You can deposit your credits into the "pot" where all users deposit their credits. Once the pot is full we randomly select a winner. So, essentially a credit is like having a slip of paper with your name on it in a hat and us drawing one user to win the pot. The more credits you have in the pot, the better chance you have to win

- the winner is drawn when the pot reaches 100% capacity
