Nightlife App

This is contract work I did as a product manager which was drawing on my experience in the industry with Connect Crimson (after my noncompete expired, of course). The product is an app that allows bars to advertise their specials to users. Employees of the bar, usually bouncers, ask patrons to check-in (Foursquare style) to the establishment for special deals and offers, or they can choose to pay cover instead. This allows bar owners to see certain data about their patrons (provided by user upon app signup) to provide insight so they can more appropriately cater to their customers.

I was tasked with building their signup page and an "as simple and straightforward as possible" form for bar owners and managers to update their bar information and specials. I also lead the product for the user data analysis page and functionality.

**Nightlife App is not the real name of this company, but was used to conceal identity while company is fundraising and operating in stealth/pre-launch. I have no affiliation with this company, other than the aforementioned contract work**
